Investment Philosophies & Strategies

Each firm operates with a distinct approach to identifying and supporting pre-seed ventures. Understanding these philosophies helps founders align their pitch and expectations.

Venture-Media (Bulletpitch)

Leverages media (newsletters, events) for deal flow, community building, and founder visibility. Believes the next unicorns will be found via media channels. Invests through the Bulletpitch+ syndicate.

Customer-Obsessed & Culture-Led (Hannah Grey)

Thesis-driven, using "Culture Vibrations" to identify trends. Backs founders redefining everyday experiences with a deep understanding of their customers. Human-centric approach.

Anti-Thematic & Founder-First (Founder Collective)

Composed of ex-founders, they are open to "weird & wonderful" ideas, prioritizing the founder over trends. Emphasizes "efficient entrepreneurship" – raising minimal capital.

B2B SaaS Specialization (Forum Ventures)

"For Founders by Founders" focused solely on B2B SaaS. Offers an accelerator ("fractional co-founder") and an AI Venture Studio. Values deep domain expertise and distribution advantages.

Network-Driven B2B Growth (NextGen VP)

Believes "Connection is the most powerful catalyst for growth." Leverages its "VP Engine" – an extensive network of 1800+ executives – for sourcing, diligence, and portfolio support in B2B startups.

Key Sector Interests

While some investors are agnostic, specific sectors often emerge as areas of deep interest and expertise.

Prominent Themes:

  • B2B SaaS: A strong focus for Forum Ventures and NextGen VP, covering various verticals including AI, e-commerce enablement, supply chain, and vertical SaaS.

  • Healthcare & Wellness: Interest from Hannah Grey (redefining experiences), Forum Ventures (operational efficiency, access to care), and NextGen VP (Healthtech).

  • Applied AI: Forum Ventures has a dedicated AI studio. Hannah Grey explores AI ethics and applications. Other firms are also investing in AI-driven solutions.

  • FinTech: NextGen VP and Hannah Grey list FinTech as a focus. Bulletpitch has invested in areas like Junior Roth IRAs.

  • Founder-Driven/Agnostic: Bulletpitch and Founder Collective maintain a broader, industry-agnostic or anti-thematic approach, prioritizing the founder and idea.

This highlights common areas of interest. Founders should always research a specific firm's current theses and portfolio for the most up-to-date alignment.
